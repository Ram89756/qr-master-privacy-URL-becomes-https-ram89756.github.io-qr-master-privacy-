# Privacy Policy for QR Master Pro

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **QR Master Pro** ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
QR Master Pro is fully free and requires no account or personal details (name, email, etc.). All QR scans, history, and generated codes are stored **locally on your device only** — we do not send this data to any server.

We may collect limited non-personal data, including:
- **Usage Data:** Number of scans, QR generations, feature interactions.
- **Device Information:** Model, OS version, unique device identifiers.
- **Advertising IDs:** Android Advertising ID (GAID) or iOS IDFA for ad serving.

## 2. Third-Party Service Providers
We use **Google AdMob** for advertisements. AdMob may collect data (e.g., device IDs, approximate location from IP, app interactions) for personalized/non-personalized ads, analytics, and fraud prevention.

See Google's Mobile Ads SDK data practices: [https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy/play-data-disclosure](https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy/play-data-disclosure)  
Google's general Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 3. How We Use Information
We use collected data to:
- Operate and improve the app.
- Serve ads via AdMob.
- Analyze usage patterns for performance and abuse prevention.

AdMob uses data for relevant ad delivery and measurement. We do not sell or share your data beyond AdMob requirements.

## 4. Ads and Your Choices
The app is ad-supported. You consent to ad-related data collection by using it.  
- Manage personalized ads via device settings ("Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android / "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS).
- Watch a rewarded ad to disable all ads for 24 hours.

## 5. Data Security
We use reasonable measures to protect data, but no transmission or storage method is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this policy. Check this page periodically.
