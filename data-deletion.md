# Data Deletion Request - QR Master Pro

If you want to request deletion of any data collected through QR Master Pro (primarily anonymized data via Google AdMob for advertising):

1. Email us at: **qrmaster.dev@gmail.com**  
   Subject: "Data Deletion Request - QR Master Pro"

2. Include:
   - Your device Advertising ID (find in Android Settings → Google → Ads → Your Advertising ID)
   - Approximate details of your use (optional)

We will acknowledge your request within 30 days and coordinate with Google/AdMob if required.

Note: The app stores no personal data on servers — all QR history is local to your device.
